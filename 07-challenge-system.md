# Challenge System

## MVP Strategy

Use a curated static challenge bank.

The challenge bank gives consistency, speed, and quality.

## Challenge Selection Rules

When creating today's entry:

1. Load all existing entries.
2. Identify the last 3 generated challenges.
3. Pick a random challenge from the bank excluding the last 3 if possible.
4. Store selected challenge in today's DailyEntry.

## Challenge Requirements

Every challenge must:

- take under 60 seconds
- be possible at a desk or near a desk
- require no equipment
- be safe for most people
- be one sentence
- be direct
- not require emotional vulnerability
- not require going outside by default
- not require contacting someone by default

## Categories

MVP categories:

- movement
- eyes
- breathing
- desk-reset
- screen-reset
- tiny-admin

## Initial Challenge Bank

Movement:

- Stand up for 10 seconds.
- Roll your shoulders once.
- Stretch your arms overhead.
- Take five steps away from your desk.
- Touch your toes or reach toward them.
- Shake out your hands.
- Stand on one foot for 10 seconds.
- Do one slow neck roll.
- Walk to the nearest doorway and back.
- Stretch your wrists for 10 seconds.

Eyes:

- Look away from your screen for 20 seconds.
- Look at something far away.
- Close your eyes for 10 seconds.
- Blink slowly five times.
- Rest your eyes and breathe once.

Breathing:

- Take three slow breaths.
- Exhale longer than you inhale three times.
- Sit still for 20 seconds.
- Drop your shoulders and breathe once.
- Take one quiet pause.

Desk Reset:

- Move one thing on your desk.
- Throw away one piece of clutter.
- Straighten one object near you.
- Wipe one small surface.
- Put one thing back where it belongs.

Screen Reset:

- Close one unused tab.
- Hide one distracting window.
- Clear one desktop item.
- Quit one app you are not using.
- Minimize everything except what matters.

Tiny Admin:

- Rename one messy file.
- Delete one unnecessary screenshot.
- Archive one old email.
- Reply to one tiny message.
- Write down the next thing you are doing.

## Challenge Copy Rules

Do:

- use direct verbs
- keep it short
- keep it doable now

Avoid:

- "try to"
- "consider"
- "reflect deeply"
- "plan your"
- "for 10 minutes"
